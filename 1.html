 COVID 19 ~ CORONA VIRUS DISEASE 2019

 

 Origin Of COVID:

Coronaviruses are a big family of different viruses. Some of them cause the common cold in people. Others infect animals, including bats, camels, and cattle.

The Coronavirus was first detected in Wuhan, China, in late 2019 and has set off a global pandemic.

 Difficulty faced due to COVID 19 Lock Down:

  • People are unable to perform thier day to day activities
  • People are unable to go to jobs due to fear that they would get affected by Corona Virus
  • People who work for daily wages suffer without money and hence, leads to no food , povery and death!

 Precautions to get rid of COVID 19:

  • Stay in home isolation for 14 days after returning to India, sleeping in a separate room, limiting contact with other family members and avoiding visitors.
  • Cover their nose while coughing and sneezing.
  • Avoid close contact with anyone with cold or flu-like symptoms, maintaining a distance of at least 1 metre.
  • Wear Mask while moving out
  • Wash your hands then and after
